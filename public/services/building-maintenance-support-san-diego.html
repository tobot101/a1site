Building Support FAQ

Questions that help clarify ongoing property support needs.

Is this different from office cleaning?

Yes. Office cleaning is more focused on workplace interiors, while this page is better for broader shared-area and property-upkeep needs.

Can I still use this page for a smaller property?

Yes, especially if the job covers several common areas or recurring upkeep needs rather than one room.

What makes the quote process easier?

A clear list of the spaces involved, the desired frequency, and the property type are the most useful starting details.

Can building support be bundled with commercial cleaning?

Yes. If the property needs multiple cleaning scopes, include them together so the follow-up reflects the full job.
